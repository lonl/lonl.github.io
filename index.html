Bio

I obtained my B.E. (2013), M.E. (2016) and Ph.D. (2023) from Northwestern Polytechnical University in Pattern Recognition and Intelligent Systems. My research interests include computer vision, machine learning, 3D analysis, UAV, artificial intelligence and SLAM.

Long, long had been my road and far, far was the journey; I will go up and down to seek my heart's desire.
